Gabble Chat
Node JS, Sequelize, express, sessions

Twitter-like express application. Users, "gabs", and likes are stored in three separate databases and cross-referenced by use of foreign keys. Likes are stored on a through table. User can create a login, save a gab of up to 140 characters, and like gabs, as well as see a list of who has like a gab. User can also delete their own gab. Completed Summer 2017
